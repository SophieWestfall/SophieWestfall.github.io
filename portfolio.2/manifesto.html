Manifesto:

This manifesto has two parts. Part I, showing the actual words to read the full, self written manifesto. Part II, this is the desing shown that, this manifesto, made not be for everyone, through the warping of the manifesto itself.

"It is perfectly acceptable to not be okay every day. Putting yourself first is not the end of the world. It is okay to make other people feel like they are on top of the world as long as you don’t forget about yourself and your own health along the way. Make art everyday. Be proud of your paintings and designs. Learn from mistakes and make sure to share your work and not keep it to yourself. It is an honor to be considered an artist, therefore, never loose your passion. Appreciate the art around you and use it to grow. Work towards your future every day. Notice your own accomplishments and changes as much as you notice others people’s. Never take your talent for granted. Limit the use of negative words in your vocabulary and gain more positive outlook on life. Don’t invalidate anyone’s feelings or ideas. Think and observe before making an assumption. Don’t take the people around you for granted and appreciate everyone you have in your life. Be happy. Be considerate. Be nice.""
