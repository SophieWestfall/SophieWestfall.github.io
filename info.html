Washington Square Park:

Living in New York can be hectic. It feels like everyone and everything is moving at the speed of light. Sometimes this means we may just need a day to ourself in the park.

This park is full of New Yorkers and tourists all year round! Whether you are having a social day where you dont mind watching skateboarders do circles around the fountain or you rather sit under the trees and relax in the shade, this website can have you naviagte where to go.

If you have never been to Washington Square before, using this map helps you learn your way around. Use the heating colors to guide you around clusters of people. The deeper the color, the more saturated the color means more people.
